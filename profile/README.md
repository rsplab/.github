# Robot Servicing and Planning Lab (RSPLab)

We are RSPLab from the School of Mechanical Enginnering and Automation at Beihang University. Our research interests include:
- Medical robots
- Service robots
- Robot motion and path planning
- Imitation learning
- Embodied AI
